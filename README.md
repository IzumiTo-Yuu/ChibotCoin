# Chibot
Chibot is a cryptocurrency that tells the digital world in the form of a robot called chibot.

Chibot introduces a universe called Chibot that contains robots that tell memes of activities in the chibot world, which has a strong identity style towards memes, making trends widely spread.

Chibot is the first meme coin to develop a universe within its meme, and allows not just a momentary trend and then loses direction. The Chibot meme universe will continue with each series. Chibot was created in opposition to the meme coin world that often carries the narrative or words "to the moon" or "rocket" without a clear storyline and continuously, which makes visitors feel bored.
